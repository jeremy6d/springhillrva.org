This is our opportunity to discuss our concerns with him.  On our agenda:<ul><li><a href="/2012/07/26/neighborhood-concerns-about-the-richmond-overlook-development/">Richmond Overlook Apartments</a></li><li>City View Apartments Special Use Permit</li><li>James River access problems</li><li>Parking</li><li>Crime problem areas</li><li>Sidewalks/storm drain/curbs (or lack thereof)</li><li>Tricycle Gardens + 21st St lot</li><li>Adjacent parcels (Allegheny warehouse, neighborhood edge conditions)</li><li>Storefront community design</li></ul></p>
