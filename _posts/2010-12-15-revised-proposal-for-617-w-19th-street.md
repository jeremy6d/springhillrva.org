<p>The <a href="/2010/12/emergency-neighborhood-meeting-tomorrow/">neighborhood meeting</a> on Saturday was a great success. The developer and architect with Daniel &amp; Company, Inc. were very open to neighborhood concerns and constructive criticism as they explained their plans for the properties at 615, 617, and 619 W. 19th St.</p><p>They intend to renovate 615 and 619 in a fashion fitting to the neighborhood, cleaning up much of the old trees and deteriorating structures on the property. On the lot at 617 they are proposing a more modern design for a house and brought a <a href='http://springhillrva.6thdensity.net/files/2010/12/617-W-19-draft-proposal.pdf'>draft rendering</a> to show the neighborhood. Incorporating feedback from the community, the architect has recently shared a new proposed design with us: <figure><a href="http://springhillrva.6thdensity.net/files/2010/12/1038_617-w.19th-v4.jpg"><img src="/wp-content/uploads/2010/12/1038_617-w.19th-v4-300x149.jpg" alt="617 W 19th St, Version 4" width="300" height="149" class="size-medium wp-image-100" /></a><figcaption>617 W 19th St, Version 4</figcaption></figure> </p><p>I hope this meeting marks the start of a great addition to our neighborhood. Thanks to Mr. Daniels, the architect, and all my neighbors ensuring this neighborhood continues to improve!</p><h3>UPDATE (8:40 PM)</h3><p>We just received updated plans for the 617 W. 19th St proposal have been provided me and can be found <a href="http://springhillrva.6thdensity.net/files/2010/12/1038_west-19th-street-12.15.2010.pdf">here</a>. This new package includes floor plans as well as front, side, and back angle views. This is what will be going in front of the Committee of Architectural Review (CAR) for approval.</p><p>I will be writing a letter on behalf of the neighborhood expressing our position to CAR. If you have any comments or concerns you'd like included in this letter, please post them here in the comments or <a href="/contact-us/">use the contact form</a>. We must have all input by Thursday evening so we can get the letter out in time for the CAR meeting. Thanks!</p>
